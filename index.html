100% Open Source

Every line of code is yours to inspect, modify, and improve. Audit security, customize workflows, and contribute back to the community. No black boxes, no vendor lock-in—just complete transparency.

Complete Banking Suite

Everything your SACCO needs in one platform: loan management, deposit accounts, savings tracking, share capital, general ledger, and comprehensive reporting. Built specifically for cooperative financial institutions.

Your Infrastructure, Your Rules

Deploy on-premises for maximum data sovereignty, or choose cloud hosting for flexibility. Full control over your financial data, compliance, and security policies. Your infrastructure, your rules.

Free Forever

Zero licensing fees. Zero subscriptions. Zero hidden costs. Nucleus is free today and will always be free. This is community-driven banking—invest your budget in serving your members, not software licenses.

Why Choose Nucleus

Built by the community, for the community. Enterprise-grade banking software that embodies the principles of community-driven banking and empowers your community.

01

Complete Code Transparency

Inspect every line of code, audit security yourself, and see exactly how your financial data is handled. No proprietary secrets, no hidden processes. When you can see everything, you can trust everything.

02

Built for Cooperative Banking

Every feature is designed around SACCO operations: member-centric loan processing, flexible savings products, share capital management, and cooperative governance tools. Not adapted from commercial banking—built from the ground up for cooperatives.

03

Own Your Financial Data

Host on your servers, in your country, under your control. Meet local compliance requirements, implement your security policies, and ensure your members' data never leaves your infrastructure. Complete data sovereignty.

04

Freedom to Customize

Modify workflows, add features, integrate with local systems—all without asking permission. The code is yours to adapt. Switch vendors, change hosting, or fork the project. You're never trapped.

05

Invest in Members, Not Licenses

Every resource saved on software licensing goes back to serving your members. This is the essence of community-driven banking—lower loan rates, better savings returns, or community investments. Nucleus is free forever—your budget stays focused on what matters.
